DECIDOPHOBIA

Decidophobia is the Fear of Making decisions, or more probably the fear of making mistakes. It is most commonly associated with a person who has a 'perfectionist' thinking style and can often lead to what we call 'paralysis by analysis', which is the paradoxical outcome of not making ANY decisions at all. Decision anxiety is real for many people!
